Key TakeawaysMarine-grade plywood is the "gold standard" for bathroom cabinet boxes due to its superior moisture resistance.Floating (wall-mounted) vanities are the leading 2026 trend for making small bathrooms feel larger.Quartz is the top countertop choice because it is non-porous and resists fungi and mold.Proper ventilation and high-density finishes (like thermofoil or lacquer) are mandatory to prevent swelling.Managing Moisture in the "Wet Zone"The primary enemy of bathroom furniture is steam. If the base material absorbs water, it will swell, crack, and peel over time.
